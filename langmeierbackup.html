Langmeier Backup

This was one of the most in depth projects I have ever tackled all by myself. The assignment was fairly simple, all we would have to do is find a website and give it a makeover. When I found the website, it was really nothing to write home about. It was none responsive, it was extremely complicated to manuever, and overall it just wasn't all that good.

I was able to go in and really give this site a complete makeover. One of the keys aspects that I really wanted to nail was simplifying the overall layout of the site. Before I had multiple people try to find the "About Us" page. Something that was deemed pretty neccessary of a company that you are going to trust with your data. I made that way more easily accessible, as well as simplifying other aspects, like the products page.

Really I am very happy with how this site turned out. While I do think (know) that there is room for improvement, this site really did turn out how I wanted it, and I learned quite a bit along the way.
