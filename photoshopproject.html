Photoshop Project

Along with my work with web design, I have had a decent amount of practice with most of the Adobe Creative Suite. I have experience in Illustrator, XD, and Photoshop.

This project is one of my favorite projects I ever worked on in Adobe Photoshop. The goal was to create a movie poster and there were no instructions other than that. I really like the way my poster came out and I think it shows a good understanding of many different aspects of what Photoshop can do.

The thing that really challenged me in this design was the background of the stars. I really didn’t want to just get a background of stars and slap it in there, so I created the stars artificailly by manipulating the grain on a background of flat black. I think it really works well and I am very proud of it.
