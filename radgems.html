Radgems

This is a site that I worked on in conjunction with my lovely wife Marrin for her jewelry business. After jumping around from different website hosts for a while, we finally landed with Shopify and were able to get this site just the way we both wanterd.

I took the reigns on this site for the most part. Marrin told me the basics of what she wanted and I made it look that way. While Marrin would come up with the content such as the pictures and the overall asthethic, I was tasked with translating that into a good website.

Shopify makes it really simple to make a good site the way you would want it. One of the reasons why Shopify is so great is they allow you a lot of freedoms to make tweaks to the code while still giving you a lot of help on the front end of things. One example of that being Order Notes. If you want to add a field to add notes to your products, you would have to pay for an extension. However, because they let you customize the code, I was able to make a custom field and bypass the paid extension.
